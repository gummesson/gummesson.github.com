Powering down Google Reader →

Posted on March 14, 2013 in Web

Even though I’ve tried to trim down my subscriptions I’m still a big fan of Google Reader and it’s a shame that they’re pulling the plug on it. I switched to Feedly earlier today and it seems to do the job fairly well. The iOS app is beautiful too but I’m not completely sold on the whole magazine-like interface. The intergration with other web services is pretty amazing though.
